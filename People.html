Player characters

Cloudstorm
At 12 cloud was sent to a college to learn more about the world, as she loved learning above everything. She spent the next several years learning everything she could, and specializing in languages.
Five days before her 20th birthday, she had ventured into a forest to collect some alchemical ingrediends for one of the professors, and was consequently caught in an unexpected storm. When it had passed she had gotten so lost she could not find her way home, so she set out to find it, using her knowledge to survive. On the fifth day, the day of her birthday, she happened upon an abnormally large rabbit, hoping to catch it for food, she hunted it, for she was aching with hunger.
She was so set on her hunt that she didnt notice the rabbit leading her somewhere, only when it turned into a huge bearlike creature and actually told her it had led her out of the forest did she notice. He congratulated her on her birthday, and said he would teach her the ways of the druids, for he had sensed the powers of one awakening within her, and that it was the reason he had helped her. Cloud accepted, and for the next year she all but abandoned her life at the college, and spent her time training as a druid.
On her 21st birthday, her teacher, Magnus the Bugbear, sent her out to travel the world, reccommending she visit the Blacklands first. He would not awnser why, only that it was where she should go. She was gifted a horse by the college for easier travel, and she set off.
Played by Mathilde Sofie Nordahl.
"Jhon"
Exactly who, or even what "Jhon" is, is indeed a most mysterios topic. He conceals his face within a hood, and keeps his cards close to his chest at all times. What are the true motivations of this mysterious wanderer, will we ever know?
He is slow to trust but quick to action, and uses a bow with impressive accuracy.
He has taken particular interest in the local occult, and has his interests fixed on a group called "the sight".
Played by Bendik Steffensen. Very edgy
Adrie Thiltana
Being born in a family line of musicians and instrument makers, it was only natural for Adrie to pursue her love for music. When she was born, her family made her a violin that she would receive on her 100th birthday, when she’s considered a full grown elf. She was brought up learning the violin, the accordion and pan flute.
Wanting to preform around the world so everyone could hear her music, she started traveling. She has played in dimly lit inns, in back allies, in congregations, even a short stint as a drummer aboard a slave galley. Even though she never garnered any fame, she yet somehow managed to make a living nonetheless.
Adrie used to never really change into her summer and winter aspect, as she’s always been a happy gal, but with the recent war and famine, she’s been liable to transforming between all four seasons. One fateful night, as she was sining and playing, venting her frustration of this wretched world, something happened. Her magical gifts was revealed to her, and she’s practiced her magic since. And now, with darker times approaching, she's started carrying weapons, never knowing when she’ll need to use them.
Played by Kristin Bjørstad.

Nonplayer characters

Oleg Thanatos
Oleg Thanatos was the grandmaster of the academy. An old and rueful man, his magical mastery is second to none. He offers help and protection to everyone who comes to his academy to escape the clutches of the Baron.
Thanatos was the only thing that stands between Baron Black and complete dominion of the blacklands, as well as the extinction of all its magic users. Rumor has it, he owns a pet vulture. Rumor also has it, that’s one of the more normal things he owns.
Oleg was executed by Baron Black's garrison at Blacktown, as part of the daily witch burnings. Since then his supporters have taken to wearing small green rags as a sign of alliegence
Lazarix Scuttenbrax
Obscenely tall and partial towards long black coats, his skin has an odd blueish tint, and in his eyes lies a profound emptyness. He smells faintly of fish. He is the martial combat instructor at the academy, teaching the students to wield longswords, daggers, polearms, maces and warhammers, nut with a most particular focus on grapping, wrestling and unarmed combat.
He was the doorman that greeted you once you first reached the academy. He is loyal towards Oleg, refusing to lie to him, never the less willing to voice his opinion, sometimes quite firmly.
Janixe Belladonna
This elven lady works at the acadamey, seemingly serving some beurecratic funtion. In stark contrast to her friend Lazarix, she is very short of stature, barely reaching 5 feet. She led the party to their dormatories when they first arrived in the academy.
She was very fretful learning about Lazarix intent to fight Baron Black's forces, both at Janestead and when he left for blackstone castle.

Grandmaster Oleg sent the party to parley with the so-called librarian, so as to retrieve vital information on a magical artifact Baron black apparently has secured for himself. Unfortunatley, they mistook the librarian's lunch for the librarian himself. He is currenly injured from absorbing a fireball from the Grandmaster.

Baron Black
Baron Blackrock, sometimes referred to as baron black, was a deserter from the Great war. He now fancies himself as the ruler of the Blacklands. Him and his band of vagabonds, the Black Riders, rule the Blacklands with an iron fist. Very few dare challenge him.
He has put the blame for the red famine upon dark magic, and stirs up fear and hatred amongst the smallfolk which allows him prosecute magic-users with impunity. Wether he honestly believes magicians are at fault, or if it’s just a convenient way to gain power, nobody knows.

Peter mason was the former librarian at the academy before the huge spider Jasmir took his position.
He used to be a military officer in his early career before transition into working for the Livonian intelligence service. There he preformed the role as some sort of super-clerk, being tasked with translating documents and witness testimonies of vital significance to state security, as his mastery of language and text was second to none in the kingdom. He became librarian upon retiring from the intelligence service, as the speed of his translation slowed as he aged.
