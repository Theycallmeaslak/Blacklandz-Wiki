The red famine
The red famine happened some 25 years ago, and together with the great war, have led to a significant depopulation in Livonia. It is called the red famine after how crops would take on a crimson color before they withered and died.
The Livonian war
The Livonian war was an armed conflict between the empires of Girin and Livonia. Livonia suffered greatly and as a result, lost control over large potions of its territory. Most importantly the entire Livonian archipelago, with Hindaja, Senaja and the Blacklands.
The strangeness in the swamp
Something horribly odd happened in the swamp north of the academy. Different mirages, ghastly images, tell a tale of a monsterous figure who killed an entire platoon of elite Girin soliders on his own.
All clues about the event indicate necromancy, and they also incriminate the former Livonian archmage "Yagner" as the necromancer. It seems he acted in self defence but is necromancy acceptable even then?
Fight at Janestead
A party of a few odd thugs start a fight the barons men in Janestead. If you know anything about these scoundrels assaulting the valiant barons men, you are obligated to report it to your regional officer.
