Blackstone castle

The Blacklands is dominated by this horrible fortress that Baron Black possesses. A horrible and ugly castle carved from volcanic basalt and decorated with obsidian. It is one of the strongest fortresses in the world.
After Baron black took control of the blacklands he started the construction on this monsterous castle. It's a massive building who some say will remain unfisihed forever. Despite, even ugly and unfinished as it is, it is remains an almost indominable fortress. This is mostly thanks to its billiant placement, making it possible to defend fully with no more than a dozen men.
