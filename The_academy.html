The Academy

The Academy is almost a city onto itself. A massive complex with a dozen different massive buildings. However it has gradually fallen into increasing disrepair, so much so that these only two building are partially operational, the rest have been abandoned to the elements. If the academy is large then that it's only a secondary attribute to it's age, said to be over 800 years old.
Oleg Thanatos is the headmaster of the academy and uses his power as an archmage to protect people from the tyrrany of Baron Black that has claimed the rest of the blacklands. It is now the only pocket on the blacklands mainland that still resist the Baron.
